Puked 隐私政策

生效日期：2026年1月1日

Puked（以下简称“本应用”）是一款专为自动驾驶研究和乘坐舒适度评估设计的专业工具。我们深知隐私对您的重要性，并严格遵守 Apple 的隐私指南。本政策旨在透明地说明我们如何处理您的数据。

1. 数据收集与目的 (Data Minimization)

  • 精确位置信息 (Precise Location)： 为了记录车辆的行驶轨迹和计算实时速度，本应用需要访问您的精确位置信息。我们支持后台定位（Always Access），以便在您锁屏或切换应用时仍能记录完整的行程轨迹。
  • 运动与健身数据 (Motion & Sensors)： 我们收集加速度计（Accelerometer）和陀螺仪数据。这些数据仅用于计算 G 值和评估乘坐舒适度，不用于任何行为分析或广告推荐。
  • 相册访问 (Photo Library)： 当您主动为行程关联照片（如路况或车辆照片）时，本应用会申请相册访问权限。我们仅读取您选定的特定文件。
  • 网络与同步 (Network)： 用于验证身份、获取品牌配置以及同步您主动上传的行程元数据。

2. 数据存储与安全

您的原始行程数据（GPS 坐标和原始 G 值）默认存储在设备本地数据库（Isar）中。除非您主动点击“上传”或开启“云端同步”，否则这些敏感数据不会离开您的设备。我们不会向任何第三方出售或共享您的数据。

3. 您的权利 (User Rights)

您拥有对数据的完全控制权：

  • 访问与删除： 您可以在应用内的“历史记录”中随时查看或永久删除任何一条记录。
  • 撤回授权： 您可以随时在 iOS 系统设置中关闭定位或相册权限，这不会导致应用无法启动，但会限制相关记录功能。

Puked Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

Puked (hereinafter referred to as "the App") is a professional tool designed for autonomous driving research and ride comfort evaluation. We strictly adhere to Apple's privacy guidelines to ensure your data security.

1. Data Collection & Purpose

  • Precise Location: The App requires access to your precise location to record trip trajectories and calculate real-time speed. Background location ("Always") is supported to ensure continuous recording while the screen is locked or app is in the background.
  • Motion & Sensors: We collect accelerometer and gyroscope data solely to calculate G-Force and evaluate ride comfort. This data is not used for behavioral profiling or advertising.
  • Photo Library: When you choose to associate photos with a trip, the App requests access to your photo library. We only read the specific files you select.
  • Network & Sync: Used for authentication, fetching brand configurations, and syncing trip metadata that you explicitly choose to upload.

2. Storage & Security

Your raw trip data (GPS coordinates and G-Force) is stored locally on your device (Isar Database) by default. These sensitive data will not leave your device unless you explicitly click "Upload" or enable "Cloud Sync". We do not sell or share your data with any third parties.

3. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access & Deletion: You can view or permanently delete any record within the "History" section of the App at any time.
  • Revoke Permissions: You can disable location or photo permissions in iOS Settings anytime. This will not prevent the App from launching but will limit its recording capabilities.
